About Me

I'm a passionate Robotics Systems Engineering student with hands-on experience developing real-time control systems and autonomous mobile robots. I specialize in applying ROS 1, Gazebo, and OpenCV to implement core robotics capabilities like SLAM, sensor fusion, and autonomous navigation on platforms such as the Agilex LIMO robot.

Currently pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Robotics Systems Engineering, I bridge theoretical understanding with practical implementation—designing systems that integrate Lidar, camera sensors, and embedded platforms to solve real-world robotics challenges. I also maintain clean, modular code in Python and C++, manage projects using Git, and build reproducible systems with Catkin and CMake.

I'm especially driven to advance the field of autonomous robotics and actively contribute to open-source robotics communities by sharing knowledge, tools, and collaborative projects.
